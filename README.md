# 📌 Objective

This project performs a cradle-to-grave sustainability analysis of an Electric Vehicle (EV) and compares it with an Internal Combustion Engine (ICE) vehicle.

## Objectives
- Study lifecycle stages of EV
- Analyze energy consumption
- Evaluate recycling impact
- Compare EV and ICE vehicles
- Assess environmental sustainability

# ✅ Key Findings

- EV manufacturing emissions are higher.
- EV operational emissions are lower.
- EVs have better energy efficiency.
- Battery recycling improves sustainability.
- Overall lifetime carbon footprint of EVs is lower than ICE vehicles.

## ⭐ Conclusion

Although EV manufacturing generates higher initial emissions due to battery production, Electric Vehicles offer lower operational emissions, higher energy efficiency, and improved sustainability over their lifecycle compared to conventional Internal Combustion Engine vehicles.
